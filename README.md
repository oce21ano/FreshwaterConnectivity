The dataset includes following model output files in MATLAB format (updated on Mar 28, 2026):

Depth_averaged_Dye.mat: ‘Lon’, ‘Lat’, and ‘Dye’ for depth-averaged dye concentrations in Chesapeake Bay, and ‘Rivernames’ for the names of rivers where the dye is released.  

Bay_transect.mat: Salinity and Dye concentrations along the main-stem transect.  ‘Bay_Distance (km)’,’Bay_SalinitySurface','Bay_SalinityBottom','Bay_SalinityDepthAveraged','Bay_DyeSurface','Bay_DyeBottom','Bay_DyeDepthAveraged'

Potomac_transect.mat: Salinity and Dye concentrations along the Potomac transect.  ‘Potomac_Distance (km)’,’Potomac_SalinitySurface','Potomac_SalinityBottom','Potomac_SalinityDepthAveraged’,'Potomac_DyeSurface','Potomac_DyeBottom','Potomac_DyeDepthAveraged'

MonthlyVariations_Connectivity.mat: Monthly variations in freshwater connectivity metrics across all 12 tributaries from 2011 to 2014. 'Time', 'Percentage’, ’Contribution’, ‘Tributary’, ’DimensionConnectivityMetrics’

Note: The dye variables consist of 12 columns, each corresponding to the dye concentration from one of the following supplying rivers, listed sequentially: James River, York River, Rappahannock River, Potomac River, Patuxent River, Patapsco River, Susquehanna River, Chester River, Choptank River, Nanticoke River, Wicomico River, and Pocomoke River
